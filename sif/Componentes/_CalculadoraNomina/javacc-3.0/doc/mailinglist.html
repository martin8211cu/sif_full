JavaCC [tm]: Mailing List and Newsgroup

You may communicate with the JavaCC [tm] developers and other JavaCC users using one of the following schemes:
  • By posting to the newsgroup comp.compilers.tools.javacc.

  • By mailing to the mailing list javacc-interest@experimentalstuff.com.

  • To subscribe or unsubscribe to this interest mailing list, please click here. Please do not send email to the entire interest mailing list to subscribe or unsubscribe.

  • You can reach the JavaCC developers directly at javacc-support@experimentalstuff.com.

Please use the mailing list or newsgroup to get your questions answered. To help us serve you better, please participate in responding to questions to the mailing list/newsgroup. Please send mail directly to the JavaCC developers only if you are not able to get your questions answered via the mailing list. We get a huge amount of JavaCC emails each day and any help you can provide in minimizing this email traffic is appreciated.
